Mining Intelligence

Mining Digital Intelligence Platform

A unified analytical layer across MineStar Fleet, Detect, Health, and Command. Built to answer the question that site managers ask at 7am: is my fleet healthy?

React 19 + TypeScript Simulated Data Environment Portfolio Project
I've watched site managers juggle three separate OEM portals to answer one question: is my fleet healthy? MineStar data lives here, condition monitoring over there, safety alerts somewhere else. This platform exists because I spent years sitting in those chairs, and I know exactly what information a decisions maker needs at the start of a shift. One screen. One source of truth. Cross-OEM, cross-module.

Platform Modules

Module 01
Command Center

Fleet-wide health overview with real-time status indicators, machine availability scores, and shift performance metrics. Designed for the operations manager's morning check-in.

Module 02
Pulse Dashboard

Condition monitoring with simulated sensor data streams. Vibration signatures, oil analysis trends, temperature deltas. Early warning before maintenance events become production losses.

Module 03
Value Architect

ROI modeling with industry benchmarks. Convert downtime hours into dollar figures. Build the business case for fleet intelligence investment with data your CFO will recognize.

Module 04
Territory Board

Geographic fleet intelligence. Machine populations by region, operator coverage maps, competitive density analysis. For regional managers who think in terms of territory, not just machines.
